WHY I JOINED THE CLASS

There are several reasons as to why I wanted to join this class, here are a few

Understanding Evolving Media

As the way we consume and create media constantly evolves, I wanted to learn how emerging technologies shape these interactions and how I can contribute to this space.

Bridging Interests in Gaming

As a gamer, I am fascinated by how games serve as a medium for storytelling, connection, and innovation. This class offered an opportunity to explore how gaming aligns with broader media trends.

Enhancing Creativity

With my hobbies like cooking and cardistry, I've always been drawn to creative expression. This course promises to broaden my perspective on how media can amplify creativity.

Leveraging Technology

I'm eager to deepen my understanding of the tools and systems that enable both the consumption and creation of impactful media.
